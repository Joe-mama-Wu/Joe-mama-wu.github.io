About me

I started in automotive, working on motor systems and electromechanical actuators — building a strong foundation in real-world hardware, testing, and product development at Schaeffler.

I'm now focused on robotics actuation and system integration, especially for dexterous manipulation and compact motor-driven mechanisms. My work spans BLDC/coreless motors, throttle body systems, test platforms, and engineering automation.

I have growing interest in embodied AI and VLA (vision-language-action) systems — building architectures where perception, decision, and actuation are tightly integrated via Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and large language models.
